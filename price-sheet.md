# Price Sheet

### Pre-Engagement Services <a href="#pre-engagement-services" id="pre-engagement-services"></a>

These services help you assess your credentialing readiness and develop a launch strategy tailored to your practice.

| Service                                | Description                                                                                                    |  Fee |
| -------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :--: |
| **Initial Credentialing Consultation** | 30-minute video or phone meeting to review your practice setup, goals, and payor targets.                      |  $50 |
| **Market & Launch Plan**               | Custom payor mix, estimated credentialing timeline, and launch recommendations based on your practice profile. | $300 |

### Phase 1: Initial Credentialing Services <a href="#phase-1-initial-credentialing-services" id="phase-1-initial-credentialing-services"></a>

This phase covers all setup and initial credentialing activities needed to get your practice and providers enrolled with payors.

| Service                               | Description                                                                                                               | Fee                                                                               |
| ------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Payor Participation Discovery**     | Written report on payor panel status, enrollment requirements, and acceptance likelihood. Includes 30-min review meeting. | $75 per payor                                                                     |
| **Group Onboarding**                  | Set up your practice in the pie credentialing system and provide a 30-minute onboarding walkthrough.                      | $150 per practice entity                                                          |
| **Individual Provider Onboarding**    | Set up provider credentialing profile, request, and store documents.                                                      | $50 per provider                                                                  |
| **Primary Source Verification (PSV)** | Verification of license, education, board certification, and malpractice history.                                         | <p>$75 per provider (LCSW, LPC, etc.)<br>$150 per provider (MD, DO, NP, etc.)</p> |
| **Provider Enrollment Applications**  | Submission of credentialing applications to selected payors for each provider.                                            | $175 per payor                                                                    |
| **Follow-Up Services**                | Up to 3 months of follow-up per enrollment: status checks, portal updates, and payor clarifications.                      | Included with enrollment                                                          |
| **Enrollment Resubmissions**          | One resubmission per payor included; additional resubmissions as needed.                                                  | $140 per occurrence                                                               |

### Phase 2: Monthly Maintenance Services <a href="#phase-2-monthly-maintenance-services" id="phase-2-monthly-maintenance-services"></a>

Ongoing support and compliance management to keep your providers and practice in good standing with payors.

| Service                               | Description                                                                                                                    | Fee                                  |
| ------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------ |
| **Maintenance Services**              | CAQH & payor directory attestations, tracking expirables, ongoing PSV, portal updates, monthly webinar call, payor follow-ups. | $100/month per provider (Type 1 NPI) |
| **Additional Individual Enrollments** | New provider enrollments during maintenance.                                                                                   | $175 per payor                       |
| **Group Contracts**                   | Group contract enrollments during maintenance.                                                                                 | $500 per payor                       |
| **Enrollment Resubmissions**          | One per payor included; additional resubmissions as needed.                                                                    | $140 per occurrence                  |

### **Key Notes**

* **All prices are in USD.**
* **Maintenance plan is a 12-month commitment, billed per provider.**
