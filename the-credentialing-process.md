# The Credentialing Process

## Overview <a href="#overview" id="overview"></a>

Our Credentialing Process: Step-by-Step

### **Step 1: Initial Consultation**

**Purpose:** Understand your goals and needs.

**What to Expect:**

* 30-minute meeting to discuss your practice and credentialing questions.

***

### **Step 2: Market Research & Launch Plan**

**Purpose:** Give you a clear credentialing roadmap.

**What to Expect:**

* We research your market and payor landscape.
* You receive a custom [Market & Launch Plan](market-report-and-launch-plan/).
* We review the plan with you in a follow-up meeting.

***

### **Step 3: Agreement & Onboarding**

**Purpose:** Formalize our partnership and gather information.

**What to Expect:**

* Sign [Services Agreement](agreements-and-service-overview/master-services-agreement/) and [SOW](agreements-and-service-overview/statement-of-work.md).
* Pay initial research and onboarding [fees](price-sheet.md).
* Practice and provider profiles set up in our system.
* 30-minute onboarding walkthrough.

***

### **Step 4: Payor Discovery Report & Onboarding Review**

**Purpose:** Present research findings and confirm all documentation.

**What to Expect:**

* Receive your personalized [Payor Discovery Report](payor-discovery-report/).
* Web conference to review findings and onboarding documentation.
* Begin Primary Source Verification (PSV) for providers.

***

### **Step 5: Payor Selection & Application Submission**

**Purpose:** Choose payors and launch credentialing.

**What to Expect:**

* You select payors to pursue.
* We invoice for selected applications.
* Upon payment, we submit applications and start follow-up.

***

### **Step 6: Application Follow-Up**

**Purpose:** Proactively manage your applications.

**What to Expect:**

* Scheduled outreach to payors, status updates, and clarifications.
* One resubmission per payor included.

***

### **Step 7: Monthly Maintenance**

**Purpose:** Keep your credentialing current and compliant.

**What to Expect:**

* Ongoing services: CAQH, directory attestations, document tracking, PSV updates, status reports, and monthly check-ins.
* Add providers or group contracts as needed.

***

### **Your Role** <a href="#undefined" id="undefined"></a>

* Provide documents promptly
* Engage with our portal
* Attend scheduled meetings<br>
